# Market Pulse

Periodic synthesized reports on what's happening in the note-taking + AI editor space. Named `YYYY-MM-DD-pulse.md`.

Generate a new one with `/market-scan` in Claude Code.

## What a good pulse covers

1. **Headlines** — top 3–5 moves this period that matter to Noted
2. **Competitor moves** — launches, pricing changes, acquisitions, key hires
3. **Platform shifts** — changes from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google that affect the AI layer
4. **Signals worth tracking** — things that aren't yet stories but might become
5. **Implications for Noted** — honest take: copy / avoid / differentiate

## What a pulse is *not*

- A rewrite of TechCrunch
- A list of press releases without analysis
- A dump of everything that happened

Keep it short. A pulse someone actually reads beats a pulse nobody opens.
